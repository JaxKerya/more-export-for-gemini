You're all set - here's how to export your first report
  1. Open a Deep Research report on gemini.google.com.
  2. Click "Share & export" on the report (or right-click anywhere on the page) - you'll see the new format entries there.
  3. Pick your format - Markdown, PDF, Word and Reader HTML are ready out of the box; enable more below.

Welcome

More Export for Gemini turns a Gemini Deep Research report into clean, ready-to-use files - Markdown, PDF, Word, LaTeX, EPUB and 10 more - with citations, metadata and a table of contents woven in. Everything runs locally in your browser.

How to export

1 Right-click On any Gemini report, right-click and pick a format from the More Export menu.
2 Use the popup Click the toolbar icon to copy or download in one click.
3 Keyboard Press Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+E to export your primary format.

    Per-format Overrides

    Optional. Override the global Table of Contents, Footnotes and Citation Style for a specific format. Pick a format, then choose Inherit to keep the global value or set an explicit one. Affects single exports and the bundle.
